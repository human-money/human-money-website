Stable

The unit of currency transacted on the Human Money Network is the Human Dollar. The value of a Human Dollar is pegged to the US Dollar. You can cash out your Human Dollars at any time for good old fashioned cash.

Inclusive

Since the value of Human Money is fixed there's no chance of becoming a millionaire overnight. The focus of Human Money is to be a currency that any human on earth can use regardless of who they are or where they come from.

Simple

Human Money was designed to be a simple as possible. Human Money addresses look like email addresses (eg. alice$human.money). Human money is also developer friendly.

Green

The Human Money network is much more energy efficient than the Bitcoin network. Instead of constantly mining every transaction that goes onto the network miners mine for "shares" of the network. Once a share is mined that miner no longer needs to expend energy to keep the network secure.
